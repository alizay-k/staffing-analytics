# Stakeholder Memo

**To:** VP of Operations
**From:** Data Analyst
**Date:** June 26, 2026
**Subject:** Sourcing Efficiency Opportunity — $406k Total

---

## Key Finding

Pakistan fills roles 9.1 days faster than our overall
average across 5 countries (19.9 days vs 29.0 days).

Additionally, Nigeria and Kenya are performing
significantly below average — costing the agency
money on every placement compared to faster countries.

## Recommendations

**1. Shift sourcing TO Pakistan:**
Shift 8% of non-Pakistan volume to Pakistan —
approximately 316 placements annually.
Estimated saving: $375,004 per year.

**2. Reduce sourcing FROM Nigeria and Kenya:**
Nigeria fills roles 6.2 days slower than average.
Kenya fills roles 3.2 days slower than average.
Combined efficiency loss: $396,792 annually.
Recommendation: review sourcing allocation for
both countries and gradually reduce volume.

## Total Opportunity
$406,180 annually from sourcing optimization alone.

## The Trade-off
Shifting Pakistan volume requires retraining
approximately 5 recruiters — estimated 2-week
disruption for an ongoing annual return.

## Caveat
This analysis is based on 2023-2024 placement data.
I would recommend validating the cost-per-day
assumption ($130.98) with the finance team before
committing to the full sourcing shift.

## Note on Recruiter Performance
Recruiter performance gap is only 0.3 days across
the team — recruiters are performing consistently.
Focus energy on sourcing optimization not
recruiter retraining.
