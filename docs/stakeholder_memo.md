# Stakeholder Memo

**To:** VP of Operations  
**From:** Data Analyst  
**Date:** June 22, 2026  
**Subject:** Sourcing Efficiency Opportunity — $336k Annual Saving  

---

## Finding

Pakistan fills roles 9.1 days faster than our overall 
average across all sourcing countries (20.0 days vs 
28.3 days). This gap exists consistently across role 
types and seniority levels.

## Recommendation

Shift 8% of non-Pakistan sourcing volume to Pakistan — 
approximately 316 placements annually. Based on our 
average placement fee and fill time, this represents 
an estimated **$336,359 in annual savings**.

## The Trade-off

Retraining approximately 5 recruiters to handle 
increased Pakistan volume — estimated 2-week 
disruption. One-time cost for an ongoing annual return.

## Caveat

This analysis is based on 2023-2024 placement data. 
I would recommend validating the cost-per-day 
assumption ($117.41) with the finance team before 
committing to the full sourcing shift. I've also run 
a sensitivity analysis — even at a conservative 5% 
shift the saving is approximately $210,000.

## Next Step

Happy to dig further into which specific role types 
show the largest Pakistan efficiency gap if that would 
help prioritize the recruiter retraining focus.
